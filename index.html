HOLI 2018

On this adventure retreat, led by Sarah Martin, Amara Risa, and Shannon Wellins (of Out Here Yoga), you will experience the glory of India's landscape and its beautiful spiritual traditions, the joy of its people, and the richness of its cultural heritage all with daily yoga and meditation to feed your body and soul. We will begin our adventure in Delhi and then journey to Pushkar where we will experience the wild abandon of Holi. Holi is a spring festival celebrated in India and Nepal, also known as the "festival of colours" or the "festival of love".
